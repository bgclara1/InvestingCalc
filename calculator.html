Investment Growth Projection

Map your future income, estimate take-home pay, and test how different monthly contribution schedules compound over time. Compare downside and upside return scenarios, then adjust withdrawals to see how your plan holds up.

How this works

This calculator helps you map a realistic investment journey from today’s income to future outcomes. It starts by estimating your take‑home pay using current UK tax bands, then shows how rent and living costs affect what you can invest. From there, you sketch a projected income path, translate that into investable monthly contributions, and test how withdrawals or big expenses impact the plan.

You can model different contribution phases, set a projection horizon, and compare three market return scenarios. The goal is to help you see trade‑offs early: how salary growth, expenses, and contribution rates shape the long‑term outcome, and whether your plan still holds up if markets are weaker than expected.

Use this as a planning canvas rather than a precise forecast. Adjust the inputs, compare the scenarios, and iterate until the plan feels achievable and resilient.
